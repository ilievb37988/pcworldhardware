General questions

How do I make an order?

As a customer, you've got several options. Once you chose an item you'd like to purchase, you can add it to your cart and checkout with Paypal, or head to the crypto payment section where you can learn how to get your items with 20% discount from the regular price.

We've included Paypal as our primary payment processor, but you can make use of any crypto you own.

How do I make a dispute?

We're very sorry that you had a problem.

We don't want to keep you hanging or lose you as a client.

It is our best interest to help you if you have any questions or problems.

If you're facing an issue with an item you've purchased, you can:

  • - Send an email to our support team.
  • - Send the item back to our warehouse, as described in the booklet we send with each delivery
  • - Get in touch with us using the contact form.
What are the shipping costs?

Item-related questions

Where are you getting your items from?

Don't tell anyone - it's a secret.

We've done a lot of mining prior to launching this website, and we have inernational distributors for various parts. We manage to get items with priority directly from the factories. The items that are listed on this website have either been used for mining purposes and have undergone a strict check before ending up here, or are brand-new coming directly from the factory (or we just have them on stock)

How long does it take for my order to arrive?

Based on our experience prior to the COVID-19 pandemic, items have reached their destination in the timeframe of 7-20 working days

The pandemic, however, made our lives harder, and increased shipment time.

Although not drastic, it's something to take into consideration.

You can check your shipment on the tracking provider website we use

Do the items have warrany?

Every item on this website, marked as Refurbished has undergone a strict check. We can vouch it functions properly and since it's been tested thoroughly, we can safely state that the item is in excellent condition. All items have a 30-day free returns policy and a 180 days warranty. Warranty conditions apply, so be sure to check out the European Union consumer general guarantees website.

Items, labeled as "New" have a standard 2-year warranty

A warranty booklet will be included in your shipment. There you can find the terms and conditions, relative to the European legislation.

What is the warranty booklet?

The booklet you receive contains the invoice, and warranty info for the item (or configuration) you've purchased from our website.

There you can find the Terms and Conditions, returns policy, and contact information

My item has stopped working. What do I do?

If your item has a valid warranty, you should follow the steps, described in the warranty booklet provided in the shipment.

We will send a substitute item in a timely manner, once the original item has been sent back, as described in the booklet.

How do you process refunds?

There are several conditions to processing a refund:

  • - Your item has not reached its supposed destination
  • - Your item has been damaged during shipment
  • - The item you received is not functioning
  • - There is a substancial difference between the item you ordered and the item you received

Once you consider that you would like to issue a refund, you can use the contact form or send an email to our support team.

If a crypto transaction has taken place, we will use the original address from which a payment has been made, unless another is provided. Use the same email you used for transaction validation. We do not tolerate fraudsters or scammers. Any suspicious activity will be reported to corresponding law enforcement organizations.

For more details, you can check the warranty booklet that is sent with your shipment.
